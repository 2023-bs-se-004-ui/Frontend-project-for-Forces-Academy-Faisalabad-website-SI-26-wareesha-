How to Apply

Five simple steps from application to enrollment

  1. 1
    Submit Application

    Fill out the online or on-campus admission form along with the required documents.

  2. 2
    Entrance Assessment

    Candidates appear for a grade-appropriate written test in English, Maths and General Knowledge.

  3. 3
    Interview

    A short interview with the candidate and parents to understand fit and expectations.

  4. 4
    Document Verification

    Original documents are verified by the admissions office before final approval.

  5. 5
    Fee Submission & Confirmation

    Pay the admission fee to confirm the seat and receive your enrollment pack.

Eligibility Criteria

What we look for in every applicant

Age Criteria
  • Minimum age as per grade level (school policy)
  • Birth certificate / B-Form required as proof
  • Age relaxation considered for defence families
Academic Criteria
  • Passing marks in previous grade
  • Qualifying score in entrance assessment
  • Satisfactory transfer certificate / conduct record
Medical Fitness
  • General medical fitness certificate
  • Updated immunization record
  • Disclosure of any medical condition requiring care
