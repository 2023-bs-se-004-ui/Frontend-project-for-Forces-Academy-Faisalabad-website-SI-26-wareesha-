How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to secure your admission

1
Fill the Application Form

Download the admission form from our website or collect it from the academy office.

2
Submit Required Documents

Attach all necessary documents including previous academic records and photographs.

3
Pay Admission Fee

Pay the admission fee at the academy office or through online banking.

4
Entrance Test / Interview

Appear for the entrance test and interview as per the scheduled date.

5
Confirm Admission

Upon selection, confirm your admission by paying the remaining fee and collecting your ID card.

Eligibility Criteria

Check if you qualify for your desired program

FSc Pre-Medical
  • Minimum 60% marks in Matric Science
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics as major subjects
  • Age: 16-18 years
  • Pass entrance test with 50% marks
FSc Pre-Engineering
  • Minimum 60% marks in Matric Science
  • Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry as major subjects
  • Age: 16-18 years
  • Pass entrance test with 50% marks
Matric Science
  • Minimum 50% marks in Class 8
  • Age: 13-15 years
  • Pass entrance test with 40% marks
  • Science background preferred
Matric Arts
  • Minimum 45% marks in Class 8
  • Age: 13-15 years
  • Pass entrance test with 40% marks
  • Arts & Humanities background
Entry Test Prep
  • Completed FSc / A-Level
  • Minimum 60% marks in FSc
  • Age: 17-22 years
  • Entrance test required for placement
CSS / PMS Prep
  • Bachelor's degree (14+ years education)
  • Minimum 55% marks in graduation
  • Age: 21-30 years
  • Interview required for enrollment
